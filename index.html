The Amazing Spiderman and the Case of the Role Playing Game

It was a normal day for Spiderman, when all of a sudden he was being whisked in directions he hadn't chose. It was as if he were in some role playing game and wait... what's this? he's the villian now?

It's your turn to decide how this comic plays out. Choose your hero -- the three remaining characters will be the villians. Battle it out by clicking the attack button. You must defeat all three villians to save New York! Good Luck.
